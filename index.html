About BMI Tracker

The BMI (Body Mass Index) Tracker helps educators and health professionals monitor student health metrics. BMI is calculated using weight and height and provides a general indication of whether a person has a healthy body weight.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

This tool is designed for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.
